My Story

I’m Funkenstein, a music producer and DJ born and raised in the vibrant coastal town of Biarritz, France. Surrounded by the rhythm of crashing waves, sunlit beaches, and the energy of dance, I grew up with music as my compass. From the first time I played with sound in my room to performing for live audiences, music has always been the heartbeat of my life. It's my creative outlet, my way of connecting with people, and my greatest passion.

I thrive in the emerging tech-funk and Indie Dance scenes, but I’m not one to stick to just one style. I dive headfirst into genres like slow acid techno, electro swing, and tech house, blending them into sets that tell a story and take listeners on a journey.

From 2009–2010, I held residencies in two clubs in Oxford, England. Since then, I’ve performed at private events in and around Paris, including the annual Burning Night at the Moulin Rouge in 2013. Then I started mixing in San Francisco, where the electronic music scene has reached a fever pitch and organized the "Beat Brunch" day parties.

Today, I call Maui home, where I draw inspiration from the island’s natural beauty to fuel my creativity. Recent highlights include headlining a massive NYE party in La Ventana’s Arroyo B2B with my friend Martin, and countless parties DJ'ing with my feet in the sand.
